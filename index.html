Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about buying, selling, and investing in real estate

You're likely ready to buy a home if you have a steady income, a good credit score (typically 620 or higher), and enough savings for a down payment (usually 3-20% of the home price) and closing costs. It's also important to plan to stay in the home for at least 3-5 years to make the investment worthwhile.

Traditionally, spring (March to May) is considered the best time to sell a home as buyer demand increases and properties look their best. However, the "best" time can vary by local market conditions. Listing in early summer can also be profitable, while selling in winter might be slower but could attract more serious buyers.

Your offer price should be based on comparable home sales (comps) in the neighborhood, the home's condition, and current market trends. In a seller's market, you might need to offer at or above the asking price, while in a buyer's market, you might offer less. Our agents will provide a Comparative Market Analysis (CMA) to help you make a competitive offer.

Closing costs are fees associated with finalizing a real estate transaction, typically ranging from 2% to 5% of the loan amount. They include appraisal fees, title insurance, loan origination fees, and taxes. Both buyers and sellers pay closing costs, but the specific distribution depends on the contract negotiation and local laws.

Yes, absolutely. A home inspection reveals potential issues with the property (like structural damage, plumbing problems, or old roofing) that aren't visible during a walkthrough. It gives you leverage to negotiate repairs or a lower price, or to back out of the deal if major problems are found.

Buying a home typically takes 30-60 days from offer acceptance to closing, though finding the right home can take weeks or months before that. Selling a home averages 55-70 days nationwide (including time on market and closing period), but this varies significantly by location, price, and market conditions.
